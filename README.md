> **AI‑powered commute safety for women in India.** Predict danger before it strikes, react when you freeze, and bring help when you can’t ask for it.

---

## 🎯 Project Vision

In India, **56% of women feel unsafe commuting alone** (NFHS‑5). SafeRoute AI is not just an app — it’s a guardian that travels with every woman.  
We combine **predictive AI**, **real‑time voice stress detection** (Hindi/Tamil/English), and **one‑tap emergency responses** to build the most emotionally intelligent safety platform ever created.

---

## ✨ Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🔮 **AI Safe Route Prediction** | Analyses crime‑prone areas, lighting, crowd, and time to suggest the safest path. |
| 📍 **Live Journey Tracking** | Real‑time location sharing with trusted contacts; automatic deviation alerts. |
| 🎙️ **Voice Distress Detection** | Detects panic keywords or stressed tone in English, Hindi, and Tamil. |
| 🆘 **One‑Tap SOS** | Sends live location, route, and audio to emergency contacts instantly. |
| 📊 **AI Safety Score** | Every route gets a score: crime, lighting, crowd, and emergency access. |
| 🤖 **Smart Voice Assistant** | Responds to “nearest police station”, “safe spot”, etc. in multiple languages. |
| 📞 **Fake Call** | Simulates an incoming call to distract or de‑escalate a situation. |
| 🌙 **Night Protection Mode** | Auto‑enables high‑alert mode after dark, with frequent check‑ins. |
| 📈 **Admin Heatmap Dashboard** | Visualises dangerous zones and live risk analysis. |

---

## 🧠 AI Modules

- **Route Risk Scorer** – Random Forest model trained on synthetic + real Indian urban data.
- **Voice Stress Detector** – Keyword‑based + future CNN for audio emotion classification.
- **Crowd Density Predictor** – Estimates real‑time crowd levels along routes.
- **Safety Heatmap Engine** – Generates dynamic danger zone overlays.
